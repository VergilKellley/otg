At OTG Sports Management, our underlying framework is built on family values, always demonstrating to our clients the unconditional support, career guidance, life skills, and personal attention necessary to pave their own road for success. As many of our team members are former football players, we have the insight and intelligence to help our clients succeed on their path to the Draft, during their professional careers and post-career. We support each client and their families individually in order to produce the best possible experience.

OTG Sports Management is based just north of Chicago, in Evanston, Illinois. We also feature offices in Tampa and Miami, Florida, giving us the flexibility to be wherever the client needs us. Noting OTG Sports Management is a Football Family, we are there for our clients 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, available whenever and for whatever the client needs.

Our Team



James "Chris" Martin President & CEO OTG Sports Management

Former Northwestern Wildcat
cmartin@otgsports.com

Chris Martin's love affair with football started early in life. Growing up in Tampa, FL, he attended Jesuit High School where he earned distinction as an All-State Wide Receiver and Defensive Back from 1990 to 1992. Recruited by Northwestern University in Evanston, Illinois, Chris became a dynamic force for the Wildcats from 1992-1996 where he was named to the All-American and All-Big Ten Teams respectively as a Defensive Back. He was a catalyst for NU's "Dream Season", taking the team to the Big 10 Championship and Rose Bowl in 1996. He graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree that same year.

Following Northwestern, Chris joined the Chicago Bears as a Defensive Back and special teams player for the 1996-1997 seasons. He managed to keep his passion for sports alive after his football career by launching a leading sports company in the Chicagoland area.

On-the-go Sports, Inc., developed in 1998, currently works with more than 50 park districts and sports facilities throughout Chicago and various suburban areas touching the lives of thousands of youths annually. In addition, he is the founder of Scoring For Youth, a non-profit organization designed to host free youth sports camps and clinics in inner-city areas. In an effort to build a vertical platform and expand the aforementioned companies to unchartered territories, Chris launched OTG Sports Management. OTGSM has taken the Sports Management world by storm garnering instant success while carefully constructing a company that's built to last.
