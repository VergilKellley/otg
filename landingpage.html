You need a trusted partner to help guide you through the process. Though you have questions, the answers start by selecting the right agent/representative. This is the most important step and will determine the future of your athlete. What kind of industry reputation does he have? This decision in my personal opinion is not one you hastily enter into but you must be prayerful about it. This is where the game changed for us and we found On The Go (OTG) Sports Management.

We are Ralph and Tracy Johnson and our son Trey Johnson is currently a Pittsburgh Steeler and member of the OTG family. Yes, I said Family, because that’s what we are! Chris Martin and his team have been confidants and advocates for Trey since the first day we talked. They’ve led us through the questions and provided all Trey needed to go from college student/athlete to NFL professional. The organization is family oriented and they provide an array of services from marketing, leadership and character development, financial planning and performance training. Couple these with some expert advice, availability and analysis OTG doesn’t stop cultivating their athletes for the moment when a team calls! And they will call!

Trusted, Dependable and Available are just a few words that come to mind when I think about OTG and our experience. If you are reading this and walking through the process, I encourage you to pray first and give OTG a call! Though they are “On The Go” you will get the answer you need.

It is every college football player’s dream to be good enough to make it to the NFL. The reality is all of them need help to get there. Brandon is currently being managed by OTG Sports Management. Brandon was noticed by OTG his senior year of college at Arkansas State University at the suggestion of some teammates that were already being managed by OTG. His talent and potential caught their eye and they never gave up. OTG Sports Management Team was formed by President/CEO Chris Martin and is comprised of former athletes — they are known for the pride and passion they have for their clients which was evident in their management of Brandon. Chris was there for Brandon through his sentencing, release and ultimately his arrival into the NFL where he landed his contract with the Cincinnati Bengals. If ever there was a management team that you want to not only “Show You The Money” but show you the support, dedication and put the needs of the athlete first and foremost, Chris Martin and OTG Sports Management is that team.
